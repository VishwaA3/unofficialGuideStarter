Good Food Around NJIT — Student Recommendations
Coffee:

Black Swan Espresso — best coffee in Newark; lighter roast with brighter flavor notes; limited seating

Burgers/American:

Burger Walla — better than Smashburger, has student discount, vegan options available
Meatball Obsession — solid spaghetti and meatballs, student discount
French Fry Heaven — good fries and chicken tenders

International:

Smitty & Mo's — located in Gateway building (connected to Penn Station via light rail); great food for the price
Ferry Street Barbecue — Portuguese/Brazilian BBQ in the Ironbound; delicious, large portions, great price-to-food ratio. Beef ribs recommended
Mi Gente Cafe — better value than La Cocina, solid food
Dario's — Tex Mex, great taco bowls; avoid the lunchtime rush (gets packed with downtown workers)
Fukurow — ramen spot, strictly pork broth
9 Thai Cuisine — passable Thai food
Masala Cafe — by-the-pound buffet until 3pm, student discount
Sigri's — Chipotle-style Indian food
Pita Square — Mediterranean food
Tasty Heaven — Korean food, decent options
Boom Boom Chicken — Korean fried chicken; decent food but poor price-to-food ratio

Vegan-friendly:

Green Chickpea — good vegan options, on the pricier side
Burger Walla — has vegan options

Drinks/Snacks:

Intrinsic Cafe — bubble tea, sweets, lounge space
Playa Bowls — smoothies and acai bowls
Fresh Coast (formerly Ono Grinds) — poke bowls, student discount all day, 20% off 2–5pm

Chains nearby: Chipotle, Qdoba, McDonald's, Subway, Dunkin
Avoid:

Giovanni's — was good before a management change, now overpriced for the quality
The Subway on campus — widely considered terrible

Halal Guys — great value, 10% discount with NJIT ID
