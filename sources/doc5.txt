How Supportive is NJIT for Disability Students?
OARS (Office of Accessibility Resources & Services):

OARS staff is supportive and kind
Sometimes offers math tutoring from grad students
You must submit a form 3–4 days in advance for each exam or quiz to get accommodations
Best to deal directly with OARS rather than professors — they will inform professors on your behalf
Get documentation in early, well before the semester begins — don't wait until the semester starts

Accommodations in practice:

Common accommodations like extended test time, quiet rooms, and calculator use on exams are available
Most professors are understanding, but some can be unresponsive or difficult
If a professor doesn't comply with accommodations, contact OARS to intervene
It is illegal for professors to refuse documented reasonable accommodations (violates ADA and Section 504) — report violations to OARS or the Dean of Students

Social scene for introverts:

NJIT is a STEM school — students tend to be more introverted overall
Most classes have Discord servers run by TAs, making it easy to connect with classmates
Students form groups based on majors and hobbies
Joining a club is the best first step to meeting like-minded people

General advice:

Strong study habits and time management are essential — NJIT is not a forgiving school
The Honors and advising offices are helpful resources in a crisis
