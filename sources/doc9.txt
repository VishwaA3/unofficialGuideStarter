For Upcoming First-Year NJIT Students — Essential Info

Key websites to bookmark:
Course Schedule (view all sections): https://generalssb-prod.ec.njit.edu/BannerExtensibility/customPage/page/stuRegCrseSched
Highlander Pipeline (registration, GPA, advisor, payment plans): https://experience.elluciancloud.com/njiot929/
Schedule Builder (plan ahead for next semester): https://myhub.njit.edu/scbldr/

Registration:
First semester: you cannot switch classes — your advisor makes your schedule
Starting Spring semester: go to Highlander Pipeline → "Registration & Degree Plan" → "Course Registration (Banner)" → "Register for Classes"
To drop or withdraw: use the "Action" tab next to Status
You can take up to 19 credits after first semester; need a permit for more

GPA & Academic Standing:
Good Standing requires 2.0+ in both Overall GPA and Term GPA
Dean's List: automatic if you have 3.0+ and no failed classes — you do not sign up
Greek Life requires minimum 2.6 GPA
Grading scale: A=4.0, B=3.0, C=2.0, D=1.0, F=0.0
Some classes (prerequisites like Calc 1, Physics 1) require a C or better to advance
Non-prerequisite classes can be passed with a D

Tutoring:
Free tutoring is included in your tuition — use it, you're already paying for it
Schedules are on the NJIT Tutoring site and often linked by professors on Canvas

Freshman Hold:
A Freshman Hold on your account is normal — it stays until registration time for the next semester, then becomes an Advisor Hold
